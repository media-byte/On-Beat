Yoga Warm-Up

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand tall with feet together, arms by your sides, weight evenly distributed, and spine aligned.

Urdhva Hastasana (Upward Salute)

Raise your arms overhead, lengthening through your fingertips while keeping shoulders relaxed.

Virabhadrasana (Warrior Pose)

Step back, bend the front knee, and reach arms overhead while squaring your hips forward.

Hasta Uttanasana (Raised Arms Pose)

Lift arms upward, arch your back gently, and engage your core while keeping legs strong.
