Yoga Warm-Up

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand tall with your feet together, shoulders relaxed, and weight evenly distributed through your feet.

Downward Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Lift your hips and stretch your body into an inverted 'V' shape. Focus on your breathing.

Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)

Step one foot forward and bend your knee, keeping your back leg straight. Reach your arms overhead and hold the pose.

Child's Pose (Balasana)

Rest your body on your knees and stretch your arms forward. Relax your entire body and breathe deeply.
